About Us

Crafting Signs That Speak Louder Than Words

Nilkanth Radium Sticker & Signage is a trusted name in the signage industry, serving businesses across India for over 18 years. We combine cutting-edge technology with artisanal craftsmanship to deliver signage that commands attention.

From humble radium stickers to massive LED video screens — every project we take on is treated with the same dedication to precision, durability, and visual impact. Your brand deserves to be seen. We make that happen.

  • State-of-the-art CNC & Laser machinery
  • In-house design & fabrication team
  • Premium materials — built to last outdoors
  • On-site installation across Gujarat

What We Do

Our Services

End-to-end signage solutions for every business size and budget.

Radium Stickers

High-visibility retro-reflective stickers for safety signage, vehicles, and outdoor branding that glow in the dark.

Acrylic & Steel Letters

Precision-cut 3D letters in acrylic or stainless steel — perfect for reception walls, shopfronts & offices.

LED Channel Letters

Backlit and halo-lit custom LED letters that make your brand shine day and night with striking visual impact.

PVD Signage

Unique Physical Vapour Deposition signage — a luxury finish that gives your brand an ultra-premium metallic look.

ACP Sign Boards

Aluminium Composite Panel boards — lightweight, weather-resistant, and perfect for large format outdoor signage.

CNC & Laser Cutting

Ultra-precise cutting of wood, acrylic, metal, and foam — for decorative panels, letters, and custom shapes.

LED Video Screens

Indoor and outdoor LED video walls and digital display screens for advertising, events, and retail spaces.

Vehicle Wrapping

Full and partial vehicle wraps that turn your fleet into moving billboards — durable vinyl, vibrant printing.

Custom Branding

Bespoke signage solutions tailored to your brand identity — we design, fabricate, and install everything for you.
